Mii Characters

Customize and create your own Mii characters!

How to Create a Mii

Creating a Mii is easy! Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the Mii Channel
  2. Select "Create Mii"
  3. Choose your character's features
  4. Save your new Mii!

Customization Options

Miis can be customized in many ways:

  • Face shape and features
  • Hair style and color
  • Clothing and accessories
  • Personality traits
