Mii Characters

Customize and create your own Mii characters! These avatars represent you inside games like Wii Sports and Mario Kart Wii.

How to Create a Mii

Creating a Mii is easy! It is highly recommended that you follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the Mii Channel
  2. Select "Create Mii"
  3. Choose your character's features
  4. Save your new Mii!

Customization Options

Miis can be customized in many different ways to look exactly like you, your friends, or totally made-up characters:

  • Face shape and skin tone
  • Hair style and color
  • Eye shape, eyebrows, and glasses
  • Body height and weight

Mii Plaza Terminology

Mii Plaza
The main screen where all of your created Miis gather and walk around.
Mii Parade
A feature that allows Miis from other consoles to travel to your console via the internet.
Wii Remote
The controller used to point at the screen and drag-and-drop your Miis.
